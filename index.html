How to use Threshold

Your training data stays on this device. Threshold has no account, analytics, server or third-party fonts.

  1. Choose the scenario that matches the departure you are practising.
  2. Check that today's target is below your dog's first sign of worry.
  3. Watch on a camera, complete the varied warm-ups, and allow the settle breaks.
  4. Return early whenever needed. Do not stay away merely to complete the clock.
  5. Rate the whole session honestly: Success, OK, or Not good.
  6. Download a backup occasionally, especially before changing phones or clearing browser data.

Success means settled and relaxed. OK means the dog coped but showed unease. Not good means clear distress, vocalising, panic or an inability to settle.

The daily limit is a ceiling, not a target. This is a planning aid rather than a substitute for a qualified separation-anxiety trainer or veterinary behaviour professional.
